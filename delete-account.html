Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Tinu Tracker account and all data associated with it. Tinu Tracker ("the App") is a personal health tracking application developed by Arpit Rai.

How to request deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, send an email to arpitrai@gmail.com from the email address you use to sign in, with the subject line "Delete my Tinu Tracker account". Sending the request from your sign-in address lets us verify it is really you.

Email a deletion request

What gets deleted

Upon your request, we permanently delete:

  • Your account and authentication record (your email address).
  • All health data you logged in the App — every exercise, sugar, and weight entry tied to your account.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. No copies of this data are retained after deletion completes, and none of it is ever shared with third parties.

Timeframe

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 days of receiving your request, and will reply to confirm once it is done.

Questions

For any questions about deleting your data, contact us at arpitrai@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
