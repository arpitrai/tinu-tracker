Tinu Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Tinu Tracker ("the App") is a personal health tracking application developed by Arpit Rai. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tinu Tracker, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your email address, collected when you sign in via Google or email authentication.
  • Health data you enter — metrics such as weight, steps, sleep, heart rate, or any other data you choose to log in the App.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the App — saving and displaying your health logs.
  • To authenticate your identity so your data is accessible only to you.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the App for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Supabase's privacy practices are described at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. No analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers receive your information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you have an account. You can request deletion of all your data at any time by emailing us at the address below. We will permanently delete your account and all associated health data within 7 days of your request.

6. Children's Privacy

Tinu Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please contact us at: arpitrai@gmail.com
